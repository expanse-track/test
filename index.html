Welcome to Smart Budget, a personal finance app designed to help you manage your money efficiently. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

A. Data Stored Only on Your Device

Most features of Smart Budget operate entirely offline. The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties:

  • Transaction records
  • Budgets & financial goals
  • Account balances & expense categories
  • Any manually entered personal notes

B. Data Processed Externally (Receipt Scanning Only)

The receipt scanning feature requires sending images to an external server for text recognition. Here's how it works:

  • You can optionally upload a photo of a receipt
  • The image is sent to a secure third-party OCR (Optical Character Recognition) service to extract transaction details (e.g., date, amount, merchant)
  • The extracted text is returned to your device and stored locally
  • The uploaded image is not stored permanently on the server after processing

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Receipt Scanning: The external server processes receipt images only to extract text and does not store them long-term
  • No Selling of Data: We do not sell, rent, or share your financial data with advertisers or other third parties
  • No Personal Profiling: We do not analyze your spending habits for advertising or tracking purposes

3. Data Security

  • Local Data: All non-receipt data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security (e.g., PIN, biometrics)
  • Receipt Scanning Data:
    • Images are transmitted securely (HTTPS) to the OCR provider
    • The third-party service is contractually obligated to delete images after processing
  • No Cloud Storage: Unless you explicitly export a backup, your data never leaves your device

4. Permissions Required

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera (Optional): Only for capturing receipt photos
  • Storage (Optional): To save/load backups or exported files
  • Internet (Limited Use): Only for receipt scanning and (if applicable) exchange rate updates
